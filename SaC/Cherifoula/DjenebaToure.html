Djénéba Touré

Born: 2002

Highschool

The beautiful young Pheul lady used to be a scruffy little girl back in 2012. She was very courious and used to follow our group when we walked around the slum to visit families. We originally visited her family for the sake of her little brother, Ally Touré. The family thought that it would be best to include the boy into our programme. However, we insisted on including both of them into our programme and soon it turmed out that Djénéba far outgrew her brother in school achievements. Djénéba is in a public grammar school today. Her supporter continues to pay her school books and transport fees.
