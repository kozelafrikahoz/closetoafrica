Fatoumata Goita

Born: 2013.10.27

Grade 2

Fatoumata is Grade 2’s best student, with an average of 8,9/10. It definitely aids her good marks that both her father and her mother are teachers. The teacher couple have 6 children. It already takes big effort for them to school their children. Fatoumata is the family’s third child in the school. As the smaller siblings get to the age where they can attend school the tuition fee gets harder and harder to pay for the family. Teachers earn very little in Mali. Thanks to her intelligence and supportive background Fatoumata has a chance to go far in her studies. We hope her supporter will be able to follow her for a long time.
