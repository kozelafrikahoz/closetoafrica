OUR ZAMBIAN CHILD SUPPORT PROJECT HAS LAUNCHED!

October 2019

A member of our foundation, Anna Surányi has spent a month in Zambia this summer. This is where she got to know the Prambs Academy School, in which we are starting our new child support project.

The project was developed based on the three principles that our foundation considers important. These are:

- based on local knowledge,
- traceable, and
- documentable.

The essence of the program remains that it doesn’t distribute money, but pays tuition fee for cooperative children in need.

For further information about the school and the program, visit http://www.kozelafrikahoz.hu/SaC/zambia.html
